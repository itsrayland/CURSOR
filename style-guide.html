Introduction

This style guide establishes the visual identity and design principles for the White House digital presence. It ensures consistency, accessibility, and professionalism across all digital communications while maintaining the dignity and authority appropriate for the highest office in the United States.

Our color palette reflects the American flag and conveys trust, authority, and patriotism.

Typography

Typography combines classic serif elegance with modern sans-serif readability.

Font Families

Georgia (Serif)

Used for headlines, titles, and formal content. Conveys tradition and authority.

font-family: 'Georgia', serif;

Source Sans Pro (Sans-serif)

Used for body text, navigation, and interface elements. Ensures optimal readability.

font-family: 'Source Sans Pro', sans-serif;

Heading Hierarchy

Heading Level 1 - 2.5rem

Heading Level 2 - 2rem

Heading Level 3 - 1.5rem

Heading Level 4 - 1.25rem

Body Text

Normal body text (16px) - Used for standard content, ensuring optimal readability across all devices and accessibility compliance.

Large body text (20px) - Used for important statements, introductory paragraphs, and emphasized content.

Small text (14px) - Used for captions, metadata, and secondary information.
