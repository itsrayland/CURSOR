B. Technical Capabilities and Experience

1. Supply and Install Modular Offices (30 feet X 40 feet)

Advanced Modular Solutions LLC has extensive experience in manufacturing, delivering, and installing modular office structures specifically designed for Caribbean environments. Over the past 15 years, we have successfully completed more than 150 modular office installations, including 25+ structures of 30x40 foot configurations for government and commercial clients in hurricane-prone regions.

Technical Specifications for 30x40 Modular Office (1,200 sq ft):

• Steel frame construction with 0.29 ga. ribbed steel exterior finish
• Hurricane-resistant design rated for 185+ mph wind loads (Category 5)
• Energy-efficient insulation systems with R-21 walls and R-30 roof
• 45 mil EPDM roofing material with 20-year manufacturer warranty
• VCG (Vinyl-Covered Gypsum) interior wall finish
• VCT (Vinyl-Covered Tile) flooring throughout
• LED lighting with occupancy sensors for 40% energy savings
• Impact-resistant windows rated for 200+ mph projectile resistance
• Steel entry doors with multi-point locking systems and hurricane straps
• Pre-engineered for IBC compliance and USVI building codes
• Configurable interior spaces including private offices and open areas

Recent relevant projects include modular facilities for NOAA Hurricane Research Division (2023), USACE emergency operations center in Puerto Rico (2022), and post-disaster recovery offices throughout the Caribbean following Hurricanes Irma and Maria.
